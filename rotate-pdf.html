About This Tool

Sideways or upside-down pages are the most common defect in scanned documents. This tool shows you every page of your PDF as a live preview the moment you select the file. Click the arrows under any page and the thumbnail turns on screen instantly — what you see is exactly what gets saved. Processing happens entirely in your browser; nothing is uploaded.

How to Fix a Scan with Wrong-Way Pages

  1. Select the PDF — every page appears in the preview grid.
  2. Spot the sideways pages visually (no more guessing page numbers).
  3. Click or under each wrong page until it looks right, or use "All 90°" if the whole document is turned.
  4. Click Apply Rotation & Download. The saved file matches the preview page-for-page.

Good to Know

  • Rotation is saved into the file, so it persists when printed or viewed anywhere — unlike the temporary "rotate view" button in most PDF readers, which only changes your screen.
  • Rotating is lossless: page content is untouched, only its orientation changes.
  • Fix orientation before merging documents, so the combined file reads cleanly throughout — reviewers notice. See our filing preparation checklist.
